The many Faces of the Joker

Actors who played the iconic roll

Joker 1

Jack Nicholson

We cannot start this list off of iconic jokers without the man the myth the legend jack nicholson. Jack's joker preformance was masterful act. Jack's joker was vastly successful and for the first time in a long time, you actually felt the villian was portraied properly and in a way that makes the movie even more emersive. He was the first iconic joker and laid the foundation for other actors.

Joker 2

Joaquin

Second and the newest joker in this list. Joaquin Pheonix's joke! Now this being the more recent should not be shrugged off so easily. Winning multiple awards and being the first actual movie dedicated to the roll of joker is an acomplishment in and of it self. Joaquin displayed a masterful class and recieved high praises. He really depicted what the joker is all about and what it means to be the joker. It's almost like he managed to make the joker likeable at the end of the movie which can be hard to do when approaching super villians.

Joker 3

Heath

And finally, any list without heath ledgers joker being mentioned is a list that can be throw out. Heath Ledgers masterful class put him in a league of his own. A lot can argue that he was the best to ever put the make up on and become the joker. Heath's joker also can be categorized as the ultimate super villian. Your not gonna like him or what he did, but you will fear and respect him. Heath sadly passed away before the movie dropped but it was one of the best farewells in movie history.
